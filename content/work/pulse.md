## The Brief

Pulse is a personal finance app built for people who've always found money anxiety-inducing. The brief: make financial data feel calm, not alarming. Trustworthy without being stiff. Modern without being cold.

## The Solution

The identity is anchored in organic curve forms that reference heartbeat data — the brand's core metaphor — softened into something reassuring rather than medical. The motion system uses easing curves that feel like breathing: nothing jerks, everything settles.
