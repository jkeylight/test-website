## The Brief

Orbit's analytics product had grown faster than its interface. After three years of feature additions, the dashboard had become genuinely difficult to use — even for power users who'd been with the product from the start.

## The Solution

A full interface redesign starting from user research. We ran twelve contextual inquiry sessions, mapped every workflow, and rebuilt the information architecture before touching visual design. The result is a system that scales with product complexity without overwhelming the user.
