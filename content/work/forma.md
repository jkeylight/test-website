## The Brief

Forma is a boutique architecture practice known for their rigorous, material-led approach to residential and cultural projects. Their brand had aged out of their work — corporate where the buildings were raw, over-designed where the practice was restrained.

## The Solution

We stripped everything back to essential geometry. The identity system is built on line weights and negative space — echoing the way their architectural drawings communicate. Every layout decision references their process: measured, intentional, nothing wasted.
